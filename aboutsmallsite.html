Meet Us

THE WONDER YEARS

The Wonder Years is an American rock band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Since their formation in July 2005, they have released six full-length albums, two EPs, and several splits/compilations. The group is currently signed to Hopeless Records. Their name originates from the popular late 80s to early 90s TV series of the same name. Critics commonly describe the group as a "fans band" because they have such a great connection with their fanbase.

"I came out swinging from a South Philly basement, caked in stale beer and sweat Under half-lit fluorescents..."

DAN CAMPBELL

Dan “Soupy” Campbell is the lead singer and founder of The Wonder Years. He also leads two side projects — Aaron West and the Roaring Twenties and the Clear Eyes Fanzine. In 2005, he co-founded The Premier, a band that was the precursor to The Wonder Years. That same year, he released his debut EP, Split with Emergency and I.



"Expect me standing tall, back against the wall, 'Cause what I learned was It's not about forcing happiness; It's about not letting the sadness win."

CASEY CAVALIERE

Casey Cavaliere is guitarist and one of the founding members of The Wonder Years. He is also is a Philadelphia-based Musician, Producer, Mixer, and songwriter.

"I'm not even sad anymore, I'm just so tired most nights."

JOSH MARTIN

Josh Martin plays backing guitarist and backing vocals for The Wonder Years. Before Josh helped form The Wonder Years, he honed his skills in Millersville University’s music business technology program.

"Twice a week I pass by the church that held your funeral, And the pastor's words come pouring down like rain."

MATT BRASCH

Matt Brasch plays rhythm guitar and backing vocals for The Wonder Years. He helped form The Wonder Years in 2005. In his freetime he enjoys snowboarding and bowling.

"I know how it seems When I'm always Staring off into nothing. I'm lost in my head again."

MIKE KENNEDY

Mike Kennedy is the drummer, percussionist, and a founding member of The Wonder Years. He is an avid artist and paints in his free time.

"Put my past self in the ground, I've been dancing on the grave. I'm not the person that I was then, I'm tearing him away."

NICK STEINBORN

Nick Steinborn plays keyboards, piano, programming, rhythm guitar, percussion, backing vocals.In April 2012, he released an EP, Some Songs, under the name "Why Bother?".

"I've been acting like I'm strong, But the truth is,
I've been losing ground, To a hospital too crowded, A summer winding down. I hadn't seen a heartbreak until now."
